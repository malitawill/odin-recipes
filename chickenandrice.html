Chicken and Rice Recipe:

On this page, I will show you how to make the BEST chicken and rice!!

First, you will need the ingredients

  • 5 large chicken breast (or thighs, simply preference)
  • 2 cans of cream of chicken soup
  • 1.5 cups brown rice
  • 1.5 cups water
  • 1 handful of shredded sharp cheddar cheese

Steps to cook:

  1. Heat the oven to 375 degrees
  2. Dice the chicken and add all the ingredients to a large pan
  3. Mix well
  4. Cook for 1 hour
  5. Mix, let sit for 10 minutes, and enjoy!
